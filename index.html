About

Doctoral Training Centre in computational biology at BITS-Pilani, K.K.Birla Goa Campus, is an interdisciplinary training initiative for the procreation of next-generation research leaders. The aim is to equip them for tackling important challenges in bioscience by using experimental and theoretical knowledge of Biomolecules in order to boost its industrial and medical applications.

Programme

The Computational Biology DTC is an innovative Ph.D. programme that provides comprehensive training to graduates who wish to undertake research in the exciting field of Computational Biology. Students coming from a very wide range of academic backgrounds including Mathematics, Biochemistry, Chemistry, Computing, Physics, Biology, Statistics and Engineering, get to appreciate the difficulties and the benefits of what the other fields can offer. Thus the research programme helps build a candidate who is proficient in more than one field of science.
